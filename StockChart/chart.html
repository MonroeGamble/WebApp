üìñ How to Use

Adding Stocks

  1. Enter a ticker symbol (e.g., AAPL, MSFT, GOOGL)
  2. Click "Add Ticker" or press Enter
  3. The stock will appear in your list with a color indicator
  4. You can add up to 10 tickers at once

Customizing Your Chart

  • Color: Click the color box next to each ticker to change its line color
  • Line Width: Use the slider to adjust line thickness (1-5px)
  • Time Range: Click any time range button to adjust the period displayed

Interacting with the Chart

  • Zoom: Scroll up/down or pinch on mobile to zoom in/out
  • Pan: Click and drag to move around the chart
  • Reset: Click "Reset Zoom" to return to the default view
  • Tooltip: Hover over the chart to see detailed price information
