Frequently Asked Questions

What data sources does FranchiseIQ use?

We aggregate stock data from Finnhub API, sports data from TheSportsDB, and franchise news from multiple RSS feeds including Franchise Times, QSR Magazine, and business portals.

How often is the data updated?

Stock ticker data updates hourly during market hours, historical chart data updates daily, and news feeds update twice daily. Sports scores refresh every 60 seconds during games.

Can I embed widgets on my own site?

Yes! All our widgets are designed for embedding. Visit the homepage and expand the "Embed Widgets" section for iframe code snippets.

Is the platform free to use?

Yes, FranchiseIQ is completely free. All functionality runs client-side, no account required.
